MERCHANT
A wealthy-looking merchant, presents his case. He is accused of murder by one of his rivals. The evidence is thin, and you have a feeling that the man is innocent, but the onlookers seem out for blood.
DECIDE: Guilty
Add Regret to your Deck. Obtain 2 random Relics.
DECIDE: Innocent
Add Shame to your Deck. Upgrade 2 cards.
MERCHANT_GUILTY
You rule that all of the man's possessions are to be taken and given as processing fees for the court.
The crowd howls in glee!
MERCHANT_INNOCENT
You go with your conscience and rule that the man is innocent.
Despite the boos and accusations of being a “paid-off judge” you feel like you did the right thing.
NOBLE
A powerful noble steps forward. You find a note in your pocket indicating that if you decide innocence you will be handsomely rewarded. The noble barely argues against the corruption charges and gives you a knowing smirk.
You can't stop corruption but you can pocket some gold...
DECIDE: Guilty
Heal 10 HP.
DECIDE: Innocent
Add Regret to your Deck. Obtain 300 Gold.
NOBLE_GUILTY

NOBLE_INNOCENT

NONDESCRIPT
A nondescript woman with a hard-to-read face steps forward. She is charged with the crime of theft and con-artistry. When the woman speaks, you believe her, but ample witnesses attest to her misdeeds.
Both sides of the story are compelling!

Add Doubt to your Deck. Gain 2 card rewards.
DECIDE: Innocent
Add Doubt to your Deck. Transform 2 cards.
