Initial Scene ⌄
Scene
Navigating the murky passages, you stumble upon a withered figure working a small shop. The multitude of shelves are stuffed full of damp scrolls and parchment.

Noticing a patron has entered, the scribe-shopkeeper snaps to attention and gestures at some implements arranged on a desk.
1
Choice
Bloody Ink
Immediate Effect
Gain 6 Max HP.
→ Result BLOODY_INK
The scribe carefully picks up the vial filled with a bright red ink and gives it a shake, then gestures with their own hand a dipping motion.

You dip your fingers into the ink and a jolt of vitality courses through you!

Pleased with your reaction, the scribe gives a polite bow and waves you goodbye.
2
Choice
Prickly Sponge
Immediate Effect
Pay 99 Gold. Enchant 2 cards with Steady.
→ Result PRICKLY_SPONGE
The scribe grabs the prickly sponge (it squeaks) and dabs it across your scrolls.

*squeak squeak squeak*

The prickly texture leaves behind shimmering marks that seem to stabilize the ink...
3
Choice
Locked
Immediate Effect
Requires 99 Gold.
4
Choice
Tentacle Quill
Immediate Effect
Pay 55 Gold. Enchant a card with Steady.
→ Result TENTACLE_QUILL
The scribe lifts the wriggly quill and with swift, practiced movements, etches a strange mark unto your head.

The mark glows bright, then vanishes...
5
Choice

Immediate Effect
Requires 55 Gold.
