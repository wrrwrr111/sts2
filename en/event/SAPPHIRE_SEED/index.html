Sapphire Seed
ACT 1 - OVERGROWTH · EVENT
You narrowly avoid stepping on a bright, sharp... seed? Its unmistakable color gives away that it's a Sapphire Seed! Weren't these incredible seeds supposed to be extinct!?

Legends say that consuming one can greatly amplify your endurance, but what if you plant and nourish it instead?

Heal 9 HP. Upgrade a card in your Deck.

PLANT
You scour the Overgrowth and find a pot with good aeration. Then, you trek around, searching for an untouched spot where light peeks through a cracked wall. Carefully, you reinforce the pot with bark and vine to camouflage it, fill it with loamy, nutrient-rich soil, and nestle the seed just beneath the surface.

Finally, you gather dew from nearby plants and pour it over the soil.

Grow big, little seed...
Unknowingly, the Sapphire Seed attaches to you spiritually.
Thankful of your hard work.
